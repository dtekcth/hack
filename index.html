Hösthackathon

Opera and dHack invites you to a web hackathon

Gather a team of 2 and sign up for an awesome event! We meet in the cozy Café Bulten in the M-building at Chalmers on a dark autumn Saturday. The hack starts at10:00and ends at16:00. You will be asked to create something cool and functional during this day. The entries should be written in JavaScript and use at least one modern web API such as WebGL, DeviceOrientation, WebAudio or WebRTC and will be judged based on creativity by a panel of representatives from Opera and dHack.

November 22nd

Café Bulten, Chalmers

We fight for glory and honor, but you will also compete to win awesome prizes:

  1. An nVidia GTX 980 and a Tegra K1 Android Tablet.
  2. Two beats solo headphones in stylish Opera red.
  3. Two Raspberry Pi B+ plus case and SDHC card.

Kicking off the hackathon

Rich Tibbett, Specification Specialist at Opera Software will introduce the participants to the Device Orientation API, its quirks and how its shortcomings have been adressed by his recently released Full Tilt library.

Erik Möller, Engineering Team Lead and one of the implementers of WebGL and author of the WebGL 101 youtube tutorial as well as members from the Opera for Android team will also be present and eager to help you with answers to your questions.

Free pizza and caffeine guaranteed!
